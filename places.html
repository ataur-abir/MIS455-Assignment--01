places of BANGLADESH

Discover the Beauty and Culture of Bangladesh

There are many popular attractions in Dhaka that will leave you spoilt for choices during your tour. You can visit the Ahsan Manzil museum to have a glimpse of the Mughal lifestyle and head to Lalbagh Kella that is renowned for its architectural beauty.

Sylhet is home to some of the most iconic sites you should definitely include in your bucket list. You can visit Manipuri Rajbari, Hakaluki Haor, and Dreamland Park for natural beauty and fun.
